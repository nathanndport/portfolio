Content

Home page

On the main parent page, the first page you will see when opening my site, it will mostly include my general information, including my career interests, as well as my hobbies and a little about me as a person so that whoever is reading is can get to know a little but more about who I am as a person. On this front page, I will also include links to my social media accounts, so the reader may do a little more research on me if necessary. On both the left as well as the right side of the page will be links that redirect the reader to the child pages of my website. On the left side link, it will take you to a page that will give you information on my education. On the right side of the page there will be a link that will take you to the other child page, and on that page, it will have all the information of work history.

Images for the Home page

Education History

On this first child page it will include a full history of my education starting from my high school years. It will include the high school(s) that I attended, and it will also include the trade schools which I attended, as well as the college(s) I attended. The link to get to this page will be included into the home page on the left side of the page, and when clicked it will redirect the user to this child page. This page will not only include the schools which attended but will also have a paragraph under each describing what I studied at the school and will also have a link to the schools websites so that the reader may investigate the schools themselves. I will also include, if any, the schools I may want to attend in the future as well as the subject of which I would like to study.

Images for the Page 2

Previous Jobs

On this second child page it will include a full history of the jobs that I have worked for starting from when I was in high school. It will include everything from my very first job when I was a teenager all the way to my full-time construction career I began right out of high school. It will include the names of each company I worked for as well as my position title I held while I worked for that company. It will also include the duration of when I worked for each company as well as my reason for leaving the company. I will also include a paragraph that will tell my career goals I wish to obtain, including my dream company to work for as well as the position I would like to obtain. Alongside my dreams and goals, I will also describe how I wish to achieve them.
